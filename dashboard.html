FADE IN:

INT. NOIR OFFICE - NIGHT

The neon sign outside flickers, casting a rhythmic red glow across the room. Rain streaks the window. The aroma of cold coffee and stale cigarettes hangs heavy.

JOHN WINTERS (30s, disheveled but charming) sits alone at a corner table, laptop open, staring blankly at the screen.

SARAH (O.S.)

Is this seat taken?

John looks up to see SARAH CHEN (late 20s, confident, warm smile) holding a coffee cup.

JOHN

Oh, no. Please, sit.

Sarah sits across from him.

SARAH

Writer's block?

JOHN

(nervous laugh)

Is it that obvious?

SARAH

I'm a writer too. I recognize the thousand-yard stare.

They share a laugh. A moment of genuine connection in the midst of the urban gloom.

FADE TO BLACK.
